2007

We launched Iyappaa Sweets and Snacks at Toronto, Canada with an initial offering of 15 products, among which the most popular items were mixture and halwa.In 2010, we expanded our distribution to Montereal, encompassing 50+ products. By 2015, our distribution network extended nationwide across Canada, featuring 200+ products.

2018

In 2018, we launched the Amrith brand to facilitate the distribution of ghee and various rice varieties. By 2019, we expanded our distribution network to include Switzerland, ensuring our products reached international markets.

2017

In 2017, we initiated the production of oil-based products under the name of Venba .
